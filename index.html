15th June

Lunch Reception 11AM - 3PM
Grand Imperial Restaurant, Sunway Pinnacle

Features an eight-course meal consisting of Chinese traditional (and contemporary) dishes. For dessert, expect light and not-to-sweet pastries and cakes, served with a hot side of gossip. The presence of the bride and groom are complimentary, of course.

16th June

Temple Ceremony 8AM-3PM
Sri Thandayuthapani Temple, Sentul

The day begins prayers for the groom and bride respectively, with the ceremony culminating with the Thali tying at 10:30AM. Proceedings of the day will also include the Registration of Marriage (RoM), because, you know, tax purposes. The ceremony will be followed by a vegetarian banana-leaf style lunch reception.
